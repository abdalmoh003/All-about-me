Favorite Movies

Bridge to Terabithia (2007)
  • Based on the book of the same name.
  • One of the actors, Josh Hutcherson, would go on to play Mike Schmidt in the Five Nights at Freddy's movie.
Super 8 (2011)
  • J.J. Abrams' only original film (not part of a series).
  • Shot in Weirton, West Virginia
All Quiet on the Western Front (1930)
  • Based on the 1929 book of the same name (filmed in one year!).
  • Was banned by the Nazis.
Platoon (1986)
  • Winner of four Academy Awards (including Best Picture).
  • Based on director and writer Oliver Stone's experiences in the Vietnam War.
The Dark Knight (2008)
  • Reinvented the superhero genre of film.
  • Despite of all its stunts, there is little to no CGI involved.
Full Metal Jacket (1987)
  • Based on the 1979 autobiographical novel The Short-Timers by Gustave Hasford.
  • Second to last Stanley Kubrick film.
